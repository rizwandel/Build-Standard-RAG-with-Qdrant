## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please **do not open a public issue**. Instead:

1. **Email us** at `rizdelhi@gmail.com` (or another contact method).
2. Provide details, including:
   - Affected version(s)
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Potential impact
